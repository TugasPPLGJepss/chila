KIM SEUNGMIN

straykids jyp idol

Seungmin is the main vocalist of Stray Kids, born on September 22, 2000, in Seoul. He’s known for his clean vocals, super tidy vibes, and perfectionist energy. He joined JYP after killing it in an audition and debuted with SKZ in 2018. Seungmin’s lowkey smart, speaks good English, journals a lot, and fans call him the “Dandy Boy” ‘cause he’s always dressed classy and neat.
